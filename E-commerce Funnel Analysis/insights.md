## Funnel Performance
- A large number of users view products, but only a small percentage proceed to purchase.
- The biggest drop-off occurs between the view and add-to-cart stage.

## Conversion Behavior
- Users who add items to cart have a significantly higher probability of purchasing.
- A notable number of users abandon carts without completing transactions.

## Customer Behavior
- Repeat purchasers contribute meaningfully to total transactions.
- Faster time-to-purchase is observed among returning users.

## Business Recommendations
- Improve product page experience to encourage add-to-cart actions.
- Implement cart reminder notifications to reduce cart abandonment.
- Offer targeted incentives to users who add items to cart but do not purchase.
