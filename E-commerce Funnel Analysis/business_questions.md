# E-commerce Funnel Analysis – Business Questions

## Dataset
RetailRocket E-commerce Dataset (Kaggle)

## Business Context
The business wants to understand user behavior across the purchase funnel
to improve conversion rates and reduce drop-offs.

## Business Questions

1. How many users are present at each stage of the funnel (view → add to cart → purchase)?
2. What is the overall conversion rate from view to purchase?
3. At which stage do users drop off the most?
4. How many users add items to cart but do not complete a purchase?
5. What percentage of users convert after adding items to cart?
6. How many repeat purchasers are there?
7. What is the average time taken to convert from view to purchase?

## Objective
To analyze user behavior across funnel stages and identify opportunities
to improve conversion and customer retention.
