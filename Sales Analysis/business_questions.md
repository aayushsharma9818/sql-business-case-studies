## Dataset
Superstore Sales Dataset (Kaggle)

## Business Context
The business wants to understand sales performance, customer behavior,
product contribution, and regional trends to improve decision-making.

## Business Questions

1. What is the monthly revenue trend over time?
2. What is the month-over-month (MoM) growth in sales?
3. Which are the top 5 products by total sales?
4. How much do top products contribute to overall sales?
5. Which regions generate the highest sales?
6. Which product categories and sub-categories perform best?
7. How many customers are repeat customers?
8. How can customers be segmented based on their total purchase value?
9. Which states contribute the most to regional sales?
10. Are there any noticeable seasonal sales patterns?

## Objective
To analyze historical sales data and provide insights that help optimize
product focus, regional strategy, and customer retention.
