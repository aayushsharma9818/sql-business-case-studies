## Attrition Overview
- Overall attrition represents a significant percentage of the workforce.
- Certain departments experience consistently higher attrition.

## Role & Department Trends
- Attrition varies noticeably across job roles.
- Sales and technical roles tend to show higher turnover.

## Tenure & Experience
- Employees with fewer years at the company are more likely to leave.
- Longer-tenured employees demonstrate higher retention.

## Compensation & Satisfaction
- Lower salary bands show higher attrition rates.
- Employees with lower job satisfaction are more likely to exit.

## Business Recommendations
- Introduce targeted retention strategies for high-attrition departments.
- Improve onboarding and early engagement for new employees.
- Review compensation structures for high-risk roles.
- Invest in job satisfaction and employee engagement programs.
