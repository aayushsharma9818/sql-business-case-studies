# HR Analytics – Business Questions

## Dataset
HR Analytics – Employee Attrition Dataset (Kaggle)

## Business Context
The HR team wants to understand employee attrition patterns and identify
factors contributing to employee turnover.

## Business Questions

1. What is the overall employee attrition rate?
2. Which departments have the highest attrition?
3. How does attrition vary by job role?
4. What is the average tenure of employees by department?
5. Is there a relationship between salary and attrition?
6. Does job satisfaction impact employee attrition?
7. Which age groups show higher attrition?
8. Are employees with lower tenure more likely to leave?

## Objective
To analyze workforce data and provide insights that help improve
employee retention and workforce planning.
